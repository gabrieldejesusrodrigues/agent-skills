# Business & Market Analyst Agent — Detailed Evaluation Criteria

## Scoring Rubric (0-100)

### Market Size (0-25 points)
- **20-25**: TAM > $10B, SAM > $1B, SOM > $10M achievable in 3 years
- **15-19**: TAM $1-10B, reasonable SAM, SOM > $1M in 2 years
- **10-14**: TAM < $1B or niche market, SOM < $1M in first 2 years
- **0-9**: Tiny or nonexistent market; hobby-scale opportunity

### TAM/SAM/SOM Calculation Method
- **TAM**: Total addressable market. Use top-down (industry reports) or bottom-up (number of potential users x average revenue per user).
- **SAM**: Serviceable addressable market. TAM filtered by geography, segment, and go-to-market reach.
- **SOM**: Serviceable obtainable market. Realistic capture in 12-24 months given resources and competition. Show your math.

### Revenue Model Clarity (0-25 points)
- **20-25**: Obvious monetization; users already pay for similar products; clear pricing benchmarks
- **15-19**: Viable model but requires market education or novel pricing
- **10-14**: Monetization possible but unproven in this segment
- **0-9**: No clear path to revenue; relies on scale-then-monetize logic

### Competitive Landscape (0-25 points)
- **20-25**: White space or weak incumbents; no dominant player
- **15-19**: 1-2 competitors but with clear gaps exploitable by a focused entrant
- **10-14**: 3+ competitors; market is being addressed but not saturated
- **0-9**: Saturated market with well-funded incumbents; red ocean

### Speed to First Revenue (0-25 points)
- **20-25**: Revenue within 30 days of launch (direct sales, freemium conversion)
- **15-19**: Revenue within 60-90 days; requires some user base building
- **10-14**: Revenue in 3-6 months; requires marketplace dynamics or enterprise sales
- **0-9**: Revenue 6+ months out; depends on scale, partnerships, or regulatory clearance

## Revenue Projection Guidelines

### Month 6 Estimate
- Assume organic growth only (no paid acquisition)
- Use conservative conversion rates: 2-5% free-to-paid for B2C, 10-20% for B2B trials
- Factor in realistic monthly churn: 5-10% for B2C, 3-5% for B2B

### Month 12 Estimate
- Allow for modest paid acquisition starting Month 4
- Assume 15-25% month-over-month growth for successful execution
- Account for pricing optimization and upsell potential

## Competitive Research Requirements
- Identify top 3-5 competitors with URLs
- Note their funding, team size, and years in market
- Identify specific gaps in their offering
- Assess likelihood of incumbent response to new entrant
